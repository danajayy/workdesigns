The Team

Launched in October 2015 by Sanda Harabagiu, Joseph Picone and Iyad Obeid, a project collaboration between Temple University and University of Texas at Dallas. The University of Texas at Dallas team are in charge of automating recognition of critical concepts of EEG reports and the patient cohort retrieval system. The Temple University team are responsible for automatically recognizing and time-aligning the EEG reports and evaluating and optimizing the retrieval system.
