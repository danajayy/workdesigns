Mission

The mission of The National Institute of Health Cohort (NIH Cohort) project is to develop an automatic, detailed and organized EEG data retrieval system that is easily accessible to the public.

Recognize EEG events

Develop automatic techniques to identify and time-align EEG events that contribute to diagnosis.

Identify critical concepts in EEGs

Automatically recognize EEG clinical events, activity, pattern, polarity, and modality.

Retrieval system

Create a patient cohort retrieval system where users can automatically access specific relevant EEG data

Validation

Extensive and rigorous evaluation of our retrieval system with medical experts and physicians to ensure quality.
