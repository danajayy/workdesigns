NIH Cohort

Vast collection of EEG data, public for the world to see

With our expert knowledge in machine learning, EEGs, big data analytics, and software, we strive to bring a huge collection of EEG data public for the world to use to advance their research and provide more confident and better patient diagnoses.

Fast

EEGs are read with automated processing techniques and sorted for quick access.

Accessible

Our data is public to the world, supporting medical diagnoses, research, and studies.

Reliable

Results are heavily tested for validation by our software and medical expert examinations.

Organized

Sorted in cohort identification system to easily compare EEG data with other similar ones.
